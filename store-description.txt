Tired of every headline breathlessly describing AI as "revolutionary" and "game-changing"? This extension fixes that.

AI to Glorified Autocomplete replaces AI hype terminology with what it actually is. "Artificial intelligence" becomes "a computer program doing a lot of guesswork." "Machine learning" becomes "statistics with a marketing team." Sam Altman becomes "tech's most camera-ready bullshit salesman."

The extension covers 150+ terms across companies, products, people, technical jargon, and hype adjectives. Replacements rotate so AI-heavy articles stay varied and funny.

Features:
• Replaces AI hype terminology on every page you visit
• Shuffled replacement pools prevent repetitive reading
• Works on dynamic content and single-page apps
• Per-site disable list — turn it off on specific sites
• Highlight mode — see exactly which words were changed
• Replacement counter — badge shows how many terms were swapped
• One-click toggle with automatic page reload

All data stays on your device. No tracking, no analytics, no servers.

Inspired by the classic "Cloud to Butt" extension, but with more contempt.
